Inequality Timeline

1970s

Income inequality begins to rise as manufacturing jobs decline and service economy emerges.

1980s

Reagan-era policies and deregulation contribute to widening wealth gaps between top earners and middle class.

2000s

Tech boom creates new millionaires while student debt crisis begins, affecting young adults disproportionately.

2008

Financial crisis hits hardest on lower and middle-income families, while wealth concentrates further at the top.

2020s

Pandemic amplifies existing inequalities while also creating new opportunities for financial education and awareness.
